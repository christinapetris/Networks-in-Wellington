Networks in Wellington

About


While looking around Wellington, there were a couple of things I investigated which reveal connections to the internet.
For example:
  1. -More people gathering around a certain area because it gives you benefits in the online game, Pokemon go
  2. -Cameras
  3. -The snapper card system (a system used mainly for bus transport)

Seeing the hidden systems...


These systems work behind the scenes. As in people may not really notice how many cameras are around the city recording people.
By looking around to see how people are connected to the internet through various things made me notice just how much people really rely and are connected over the internet.

Pokemon Go

The game pokemon go encourages people to go places. In the game there are 'pokestops' which are locations that give items if the person is physically near these locations. They also have other benefits in the game. It is interesting to notice how when people are on their phone a lot when walking or there are a lot of people in a certain area it is most likely because of pokemon go!

This shows people gathered by the waterfront on their phones. Then the next image shows what the game looks like in that area. The game shows a lot of 'pokestops' which are the blue 3d boxes. These pokestops have a module applied on them which lures more pokemon to the area and therefore there will be more people playing the game in the area.

Cameras

I looked for cameras at different locations around Wellington.

The camera on the left was found at an intersection on the Terrace near Victoria University. The other camera is by Midland Park on Lambton Quay. It is interesting to note how this is a very public place that people enjoy to relax on their lunch break. But they may not notice the cameras which are recording them. I never noticed this camera before doing this research.
